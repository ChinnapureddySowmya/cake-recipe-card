Chocolate Cake

Prep: 20 min Serves: 8 Difficulty: Easy ⏱ 30:00

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup sugar
    • png
  • ¼ cup cocoa powder
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 1 cup warm water
  • ⅓ cup vegetable oil
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 tsp vinegar

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C (350°F). Grease a 20cm round pan.
  2. Whisk dry ingredients in a large bowl.
  3. Add water, oil, vanilla, and vinegar. Whisk until smooth.
  4. Pour batter into pan and smooth the top.
  5. Bake 30–35 minutes until a skewer comes out clean.
  6. Cool completely, then slice and serve.

Notes & Tips

• Swap water for hot coffee to boost chocolate flavor.
• Add chocolate chips for extra richness.
• For cupcakes, bake ~18–20 minutes.
